The story nijinn can own

One market category. Several different professional problems.

The strongest presentation will show the product through a small number of practical questions. Each one can become a live demonstration, a case study or a focused conversation with the audience.

When do two contracts represent the same event?

Price comparison is useful only when the underlying contracts are genuinely comparable. nijinn can show how resolution language, source agencies, fees, liquidity and settlement mechanics change the real opportunity.

Live proof: compare one event across several venues
Audience value: understand basis and resolution risk
Commercial value: establish nijinn as a trusted intelligence layer

How does a professional turn fragmented markets into a workflow?

Arbitrage, directional trading and market making each require different information. nijinn can demonstrate how a user moves from market discovery to a decision without opening several disconnected tools.

Live proof: surface a cross-venue opportunity
Audience value: see the workflow rather than a static odds page
Commercial value: make the first paid use case easy to understand

What happens after the trade?

Performance auditing and real-world hedging can widen nijinn's relevance beyond active traders. This is where the product can connect prediction-market signals to portfolio decisions, risk management and institutional use cases.

Live proof: show attribution, exposure or a hedge scenario
Audience value: connect markets to real decisions
Commercial value: open conversations with institutions and enterprises
